Reaction Lab

Wait for the signal, then tap fast. Click too early and it counts as a false start.

How to play

  1. Press the Space bar or click Start to arm the test.
  2. Wait through a random delay between 1 and 5 seconds.
  3. When the panel turns green and says GO, click the reaction area as fast as you can.
  4. If you click before the signal, you trigger a false start.

Lower times are better. Each reaction gets a grade: Amazing, Great, Good, Average, Bad, or Pathetic.
